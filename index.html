Full Job Description

About OHLMEC: OHLMEC provides project management and consulting, specialised maintenance, fabrication on and off site, construction support and structural integrity maintenance and repairs to a variety of clients across Western Australia. With a workforce approaching 100+, we have strategically grown from our beginnings in early 2022.

About the Role:

We are seeking experienced local Mechanical Fitters and Boilermakers to join our Kalgoorlie Operations. This role is part of our rapid growth and offers an opportunity to be part of a company that values safety, quality, culture, and integrity.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Perform mechanical fitting or boilermaking tasks as per project requirements
  • Work efficiently in a busy, fast-paced environment
  • Contribute to maintenance, fabrication, and construction support projects
  • Uphold and promote the company's four core values in daily operations

Qualifications and Skills:

  • Australian Recognised Trade Qualification (essential)
  • Current and valid manual Australian Driver's licence (essential)
  • Confined space, Working at heights and White Card certifications
  • Gas Test Atmospheres certification (for Boilermakers)
  • Advantageous: EWP, (LF) Forklift, Crane, and Basic rigging Tickets
  • NPC within 12 Months or willingness to obtain
  • Proven experience in a similar role
  • Ability to perform under pressure in a fast-paced environment

Benefits:

  • Attractive remuneration package
  • Opportunities for career development within a growing company
  • Various roster options available
  • Be part of a culture you can be proud of

Additional Information: OHLMEC is an Equal Opportunity Employer and promotes equality and diversity. Indigenous Australians and candidates from minority groups are encouraged to apply. Shortlisting will begin immediately, so interested candidates are encouraged to apply as soon as possible.
